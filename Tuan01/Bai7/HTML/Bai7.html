Body Systems LSM400 Linear Smith Machine

Linear Smith Machines are the ultimate in weight machines and the LSM400 leads the field.

Overview

Designed for both serious fitness users and beginners, the LSM400's vertical design provides all of the safety and control associated with Smith Machines, while still allowing for the effective use of gravity for effective resistance. You don't even need a workout partner. Lock points can be set to prevent the bar from movement outside of your body's natural range of motion.

You can tailor the LSM400 to meet your specific workout needs. Start with the basic package of the Smith Machine rack and add options as needed. The LSM400 works with all standard Olympic weight plates.

The LSM400 is compact with all components placed on one side of the unit for easy access, ideal for home gyms.

Features

  • Smooth Smith Action: The bar (rated at 800 lb. capacity) slides on Teflon bushings alongside solid steel guide rods.
  • Proper Angle: The bar travels along a 7 degree path from vertical for comfort and injury prevention.
  • Safety: 20 lock-out points for safe stops during workouts.
  • Rugged: Constructed with heavy 12 gauge steel and 7 gauge reinforcing plate.
  • Warranty: Lifetime guarantee on all parts.
